## ğŸ¯ Gameplay

Control Kami's balloon as he journeys through the sky:
- **Hold** to heat the balloon (ascend)
- **Release** to cool down (descend)
- Collect fuel to keep flying
- Avoid obstacles like mountains, birds, and storms
- Reach altitude goals and rescue objectives
